NIMMI'S WORLD


Short Story


This is Nimmi's story. The story of her success. Not everyone can see the world with a positive attitude. But she had a faith in herself that helped to achieve her goal. Nimmi was not a normal girl. Her world was silent.
Born in a middle class family, Nimmi's childhood was not so happy. Even her parents and neighbours cursed her for being mute. But Nimmi had a strong faith in herself that one day she could make her parents proud of her. Nimmi's brother and his friends always made fun of her calling 'dumb', 'earless', useless etc. But she never minded them.

Her world was full of visuals and touches. She enjoyed nature in her own ways. Drawing was her inborn talent and she had a great passion for it. She had a different hobby of copying everything she saw into a sheet of paper. That’s why there were whole pictures in her school books. Her mother often scolds her for seeing pictures drawn in books she studies.

As she grew older, her desire to be an artist grew. She started participating in competitions. None of her family encouraged her talents just because they wanted her to get a job and secure her life. For that they needed her to focus on her studies. But Nimmi was ambitious and wanted to build up her career as an Artist.
By her parents' force she joined as a teacher in a disability center. But her mind was always behind her strong wish to become an Artist. When Nimmi was 23, her parents decided to fix her marriage. But she was not interested in it. She wanted to be an Artist. So she tried to cancel all the alliances that her papa brought. Her brother was very angry with her and said that he would kill her if she didn't agree with the family's decision. She had no more options. Her marriage was fixed.
Nimmi felt like she was dead. She loved her parents and also her dreams. But now, everything is going to end. She texted about her last few weeks to her best friend, Snisha. Snisha was always supportive and wanted to see her successful. Hearing her fate she became so sad.

But Snisha never wanted to see her end her life like normal people. So she made a plan and discussed it with Nimmi. Nimmi was so happy to hear it and was always thankful to her best friend. Two weeks before her marriage, she jumped from her house and took a train ticket to Chennai.
Snisha's family welcomed her happily to their guest house. Nimmi had a wonderful experience there. She painted everything she saw there. Snisha's family had an estate in Chennai, so they used to go there often. There were many workers and therefore Nimmi felt comfortable to stay there. It was away from the town so that no one could find her.

It was the best place she had ever been for developing her skills. She could touch nature as close as possible. She was really happy there. She started to participate in art exhibitions and competitions and won many prizes. Snisha's family was always supportive. They helped her a lot in her path to success. Once, A company was very much interested in her works and appointed her as their chief designer.
But she was not satisfied. She worked there for one year and resigned her job. Then she started thinking about business. But it needs a lot of money. So she increased her work, participated more often in exhibitions and competitions. She also joined an institute where she can teach drawing to students. Her dream was an Art Gallery. Three years later her dream was fulfilled. She opened her Art Gallery Successfully. News spread faster and her parents came to know about it.They felt ashamed of themselves.

They asked sorry for their mistake. But Nimmi was so happy to see them. Her Art Gallery named herself.
"THE GARDEN OF SILENCE"
